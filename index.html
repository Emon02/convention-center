Frequently Asked Questions

Event design and event planning work hand in hand to deliver a successful event.Events by Knight offer tailor made events. This means we incorporate both event design and event planning to create that perfect event.

Planning an event can take from weeks to months, it really depends on the size and the details that the event involves. Having enough time allows you to comfortably cover different areas and prepare yourself for potential scenarios that might happen so you can consider various options prior the date. You always need more time than you think!

Cancellations made 7 days or more in advance of the event date, will receive a 100% refund. Cancellations made within 3 - 6 days will incur a 20% fee. Cancellations made within 48 hours to the event will incur a 30% fee.

Staying 100% within your budget means you have done an amazing job, but just remember there is always those last minute items that are needed so make sure you have a little bit left over which will cover most situations. If you are super organized then you could even create a list of situations you may encounter and how much you would need to rectify the problem. Creating checklists and event survival kits for event staff is much needed to make sure everyone keeps on point and knows what is needed from them from start to end.
